Orange Sun Cold-Pressed Juice

Yield: 12 oz (355 ml)

Description

Bursting with the vibrant essence of freshly picked oranges, Orange Sun delivers a refreshing splash of citrus in every sip. Made with a gentle cold-press extraction, it preserves natural vitamins, enzymes, and the bold flavor of sun-ripened fruit!

Served chilled, this golden elixir is the ultimate thirst-quencher on a hot day or after a workout. Clean, crisp, and sunshine-bright. It's just pure, revitalizing citrus bliss without added sugars or artificial ingredients.

Ingredients (for 12 oz)

  • 3 medium navel oranges, peeled
  • 1/2 small lemon, peeled (optional, for brightness)
  • 1/4 inch fresh ginger, peeled (optional, subtle kick)

Tip: Aim for ~13–14 oz pre-strain to finish at ~12 oz after filtering.

Equipment

  • Cold-press (masticating) juicer
  • Mylk bag or fine-mesh strainer (for extra-smooth texture)
  • BPA-free bottle or glass mason jar with lid

Steps to Make 12 oz

  1. Peel oranges and lemon (if using); trim and peel the ginger.
  2. Remove any visible seeds and cut fruit into chute-friendly pieces.
  3. Power on the juicer. Feed the orange pieces first, followed by lemon and ginger.
  4. (Optional) Strain the juice through a mylk bag or fine-mesh strainer for a polished finish.
  5. Bottle immediately. Chill 30–60 minutes for best flavor.
  6. Store sealed in the refrigerator and enjoy within 3–5 days.

Notes & Adjustments

  • More tangy? Use the lemon or add an extra lemon wedge.
  • Milder spice? Skip ginger or use a thinner slice.
  • Lower pulp? Double-strain after the first pass.
