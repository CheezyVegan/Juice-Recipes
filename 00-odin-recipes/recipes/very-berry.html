Very Berry Cold-Pressed Juice

Yield: 12 oz (355 ml)

Description

Juicy, vibrant, and antioxidant-rich, Very Berry is a lush fusion of strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, and blackberries balanced with a splash of lemon and crisp apple. Cold-pressed to keep delicate vitamins and enzymes intact, it pours a deep ruby hue with a bright purple finish.

Enjoy it chilled after a workout or as a mid-day pick-me-up. Each sip is smooth and refreshing, with a natural sweetness that tastes like summer berries at peak ripeness.

Ingredients (for 12 oz)

  • 1 cup strawberries, hulled (about 6 to 8 berries)
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup raspberries
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 small sweet apple, cored (adds body & clarity)
  • 1/4 small lemon, peeled (brightness)
  • 2 – 3 fresh mint or basil leaves (optional, aromatic)

Tip: Soft berries yield more pulp: aim for ~13–14 oz pre-strain to finish at ~12 oz after filtering.

Equipment

  • Cold-press (masticating) juicer
  • Mylk bag or fine-mesh strainer (for extra-smooth texture)
  • BPA-free bottle or glass mason jar with lid

Steps to Make 12 oz

  1. Rinse berries thoroughly. Hull strawberries; core the apple. Peel the lemon section and rinse herbs (if using).
  2. Cut larger fruit into chute-friendly pieces.
  3. Power on the juicer. Feed berries and apple in alternating handfuls to keep flow steady; finish with lemon and herbs.
  4. (Optional) Strain through a mylk bag or fine-mesh strainer for a polished, low-pulp finish.
  5. Bottle immediately. Chill 30–60 minutes for best flavor.
  6. Store sealed in the refrigerator and enjoy within 3–5 days.

Notes & Adjustments

  • Sweeter? Add 2–3 more strawberries or use a sweeter apple (e.g., Fuji or Gala).
  • Brighter/tarter? Increase lemon slightly or add a few extra raspberries.
  • Silkiest texture? Double-strain and let the juice rest 5 minutes before bottling to reduce foam.
