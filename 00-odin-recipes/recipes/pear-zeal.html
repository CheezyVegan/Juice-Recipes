Pearl Zeal Cold-Pressed Juice

Yield: 12 oz (355 ml)

Description

Bright, clean, and gently sweet, our Pearl Zeal cold-pressed juice blends juicy pears with cooling cucumber, a squeeze of lemon, and a whisper of ginger. The result is a crisp, spa-like sip that hydrates and revitalizes without added sugars or heat. Just pure produce, pressed at its peak!

Enjoy it ice-cold post-workout or as a mid-day reset. Every bottle captures delicate enzymes and vitamins for a refreshing finish that’s light on the palate and big on feel-good energy.

Ingredients (for 12 oz)

  • 1 large ripe Bartlett pear, cored
  • 1 small green apple, cored (adds brightness & balance)
  • 1/3 medium cucumber (cooling + hydration)
  • 1/4 small lemon, peeled (for acidity)
  • 1/4 inch fresh ginger, peeled (optional subtle warmth)
  • 2–3 fresh mint leaves (optional aromatic finish)

Tip: Aim for ~12–13 oz pre-strain to finish at ~12 oz after filtering.

Equipment

  • Cold-press (masticating) juicer
  • Mylk bag or fine mesh strainer (optional for extra-smooth texture)
  • BPA-free bottle or glass mason jar with lid

Steps to Make 12 oz

  1. Wash, then core the pear and apple; peel the lemon section and ginger. Rinse the mint.
  2. Cut produce into chute-friendly pieces.
  3. Power on the juicer. Feed cucumber, pear, and apple first; follow with lemon, ginger, and mint.
  4. (Optional) Strain the juice through a mylk bag for a polished, velvety finish.
  5. Bottle immediately. Chill for 30–60 minutes for best flavor.
  6. Store sealed in the refrigerator and enjoy within 3–5 days.

Notes & Adjustments

  • More tart? Increase lemon slightly or swap green apple for a tarter variety (e.g., Granny Smith).
  • Sweeter? Use a very ripe pear or add a few extra pear slices.
  • Lower pulp? Double-strain through a fine mesh after the mylk bag.
